About Us

For over two decades, Lakeshore Plumbing has been providing reliable, high-quality plumbing services to homeowners and businesses in our community. Built on a foundation of trust, craftsmanship, and honest pricing, our reputation has grown entirely through the relationships we've built and the referrals of satisfied customers.

Our experienced team is committed to getting every job done right the first time — whether it's a new construction project, sewer & water service installation, or a rebuild. When you call Lakeshore Plumbing, you're calling a neighbor who takes pride in their work.
